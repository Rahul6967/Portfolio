Employee Management System

Technologies Used: Python, django, HTML, CSS, Js, SQLite

The Employee Record Management System (ERMS) is a versatile and user-friendly solution designed to efficiently manage and organize employee information within an organization. With a powerful admin panel, administrators have complete control over all aspects of the system, including employee details, academic records, and working experience. The platform ensures data security through robust login and authentication features for both administrators and employees. ERMS facilitates seamless editing of employee information, providing flexibility to adapt to dynamic changes. By offering a comprehensive view of an employee's professional journey, from basic details to academic achievements and work experience, ERMS becomes an indispensable tool for HR management, promoting accuracy and efficiency in handling crucial organizational data.
